Artist Statement

My work explores the intersection between the natural world and human experience. I am fascinated by how we perceive and interact with our environment, and how these interactions shape our understanding of ourselves and our place in the world.

Through my paintings, I seek to capture moments of connection and revelation—those fleeting instances when the ordinary becomes extraordinary. I am drawn to the tension between representation and abstraction, between what is seen and what is felt.

Color plays a crucial role in my practice, serving as both an emotional catalyst and a structural element. I use layering techniques to create depth and complexity, inviting viewers to look beyond the surface and discover new perspectives with each viewing.

Ultimately, my aim is to create work that resonates on multiple levels—visually stimulating, emotionally evocative, and intellectually engaging. I hope to inspire viewers to see the world around them with fresh eyes and a renewed sense of wonder.
